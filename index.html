Yard Legend

Main anchor of the map. The player starts from the back door and future levels may extend toward the front driveway.

Door

Back door exit where the player enters the yard and brings food out from inside the house.

Shed

Smaller shed landmark where kittens may sometimes be born underneath, encouraging food placement nearby.

Water

Water bowl area that helps define a cat activity cluster without implying a permanent feeder object.

Crate

Placeholder supply or trap staging object that can later become a movement obstacle.
